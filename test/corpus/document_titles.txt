==================
Two lines before blank parsed as title+subtitle by tree-sitter
==================
First line
Second line continues the paragraph.

Third paragraph.
---

; NOTE: tree-sitter parses two lines + blank as title+subtitle because it
; cannot check whether the first line ends with a colon (CST limitation).
; The lex-core parser correctly rejects this as a title because the first
; line does NOT end with a colon.

(document
  (document_title
    title: (line_content
      (text_content))
    (document_subtitle
      subtitle: (line_content
        (text_content)))
    (blank_line))
  (paragraph
    (text_line
      (line_content
        (text_content)))))
